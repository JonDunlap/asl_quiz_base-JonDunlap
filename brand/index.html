Back-End Learning

Back-End Learning helps people who are interested in learning or improving their skills when it comes to back-end development. We offer questions and tests to help people learn more about back-end development and all that comes with it.

This app's main demographic.

  • Gender: Males
  • Ages: 20 - 40
  • Education: pursing higher education or some college/university.
